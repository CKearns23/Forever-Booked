Riley Sager

Riley Sager is a bestselling author of thrillers and suspense novels. Known for his gripping plots and unexpected twists, Sager has captured the attention of readers around the world. His debut novel, *Final Girls*, was an instant success and established him as a master of psychological thrillers. Since then, he has written a string of bestsellers including *The Last Time I Lied*, *Lock Every Door*, *Home Before Dark*, *The House Across the Lake*, and *The Only One Left*. Sager's books have been praised for their fast-paced narratives, complex characters, and ability to keep readers on the edge of their seats. He currently resides in the United States and continues to write thrilling, page-turning novels.
