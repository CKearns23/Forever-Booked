Alice Feeney

Alice Feeney is a British author best known for her psychological thrillers, which often explore themes of memory, truth, and deception. Before becoming a full-time writer, she worked as a BBC journalist for 15 years. Her debut novel, *Sometimes I Lie*, became a bestseller and set the tone for her career, with its twisty, suspenseful storytelling. Feeney has since penned several bestselling novels, including *I Know Who You Are*, *His & Hers*, *Rock Paper Scissors*, and *Daisy Darker*. She currently lives in the Devon countryside with her family, where she continues to craft compelling mysteries.
