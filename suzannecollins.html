Suzanne Collins

Suzanne Collins is an American author best known for writing *The Hunger Games* trilogy, a global phenomenon that has captivated millions of readers worldwide. Born in 1962, Collins initially worked as a television writer before shifting to novels. *The Hunger Games* series, set in a dystopian future, explores themes of survival, oppression, and rebellion. The trilogy has been translated into multiple languages and adapted into a successful film series. In addition to *The Hunger Games*, Collins is also the author of *Gregor the Overlander*, a fantasy series aimed at younger readers. Suzanne Collins' powerful storytelling continues to resonate with both young and adult audiences.
