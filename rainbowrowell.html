Rainbow Rowell

Rainbow Rowell is a bestselling author known for her emotionally compelling young adult and contemporary novels. Her debut novel, *Eleanor & Park*, won numerous accolades and was a favorite among readers, praised for its portrayal of first love, identity, and the challenges of adolescence. Rowell's works often explore themes of love, belonging, and self-discovery. In addition to her novels, Rowell has written short stories and graphic novels, and she is known for her ability to craft relatable characters with depth and authenticity. Other notable works include *Fangirl*, *Carry On*, and *Attachments*. Rainbow Rowell currently lives in Omaha, Nebraska, with her family.
