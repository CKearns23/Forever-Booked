Kasie West

Kasie West is a bestselling author known for her heartfelt contemporary romance novels, often filled with humor, heart, and a touch of magic. She has written a number of popular books that resonate with young adult readers, especially those who enjoy swoon-worthy romance and relatable characters. Some of her best-known works include *The Distance Between Us*, *The Fill-In Boyfriend*, *On the Fence*, *P.S. I Like You*, *By Your Side*, and *Pivot Point*. Her novels often explore themes of friendship, self-discovery, and love. Kasie lives in California and when she’s not writing, she loves to read, spend time with family, and enjoy the sunshine.
