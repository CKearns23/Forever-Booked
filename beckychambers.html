Becky Chambers

Becky Chambers is an American science fiction author known for her character-driven, optimistic stories that explore themes of community, identity, and human connection. Her debut novel, The Long Way to a Small, Angry Planet, received critical acclaim for its heartwarming portrayal of a diverse crew navigating space. Chambers’ work often emphasizes empathy and cooperation, featuring inclusive and progressive worlds that reflect modern social issues.
