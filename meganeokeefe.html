Megan E. O'Keefe

Megan E. O'Keefe was raised in a family of journalists, and her early love for storytelling led her to craft a newsletter chronicling the daily adventures of the local cat population. Now a writer, she resides in the Bay Area of California where she spends her free time tinkering with anything she can get her hands on. O'Keefe made her debut in the fantasy genre with *Steal the Sky*, which went on to win the prestigious Gemmell Morningstar Award. Her space opera debut, *Velocity Weapon*, was nominated for the Philip K. Dick Award. Known for her compelling characters and intricate world-building, O'Keefe is quickly establishing herself as a rising star in science fiction and fantasy.
