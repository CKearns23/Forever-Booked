Stephen King

Stephen King is a renowned American author, best known for his prolific contributions to the horror genre. With over 60 novels, 200 short stories, and numerous adaptations, King has become one of the most influential writers of contemporary horror. His works, including Carrie, The Shining, It, and Misery, often explore themes of fear, the supernatural, and human resilience. King’s ability to blend psychological tension with supernatural elements has garnered him a global fanbase. His distinct voice and knack for creating relatable characters have made him a household name in horror literature, earning him numerous awards and honors over the decades.
