Nora Roberts

Nora Roberts is a prolific American author, best known for her romance novels and series. With over 200 books to her name, Roberts has built a career spanning decades, including hits like *The Bridal Quartet* series and the *In Death* series (under the pseudonym J.D. Robb). Her stories often blend romance with elements of mystery, suspense, and fantasy. Known for her richly developed characters and vivid settings, Nora Roberts has earned multiple awards and is considered one of the most successful and influential authors in the romance genre.
