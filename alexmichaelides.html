Alex Michaelides

Alex Michaelides is a British author known for his psychological thrillers. His debut novel, *The Silent Patient*, became an international bestseller, praised for its unexpected twists and psychological depth. Michaelides excels in creating complex characters and weaving intricate plots that captivate readers. His writing delves deeply into themes of trauma, guilt, and the darker aspects of the human psyche. His second novel, *The Maidens*, follows suit with a gripping blend of mystery and psychological suspense, cementing his reputation as a master of the genre.
