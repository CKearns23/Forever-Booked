Ann Leckie

Ann Leckie is an American author best known for her *Imperial Radch* series, which includes her debut novel *Ancillary Justice*. The book won multiple prestigious awards, including the Hugo, Nebula, and Arthur C. Clarke Awards. Leckie’s work explores themes of identity, consciousness, and the role of power in society, often set in far-future, space-faring civilizations. Her writing blends space opera with deep philosophical questions, featuring complex characters and innovative explorations of gender. Leckie’s works are celebrated for their intricate plotting and world-building.
