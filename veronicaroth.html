Veronica Roth

Veronica Roth is a New York Times bestselling author best known for her *Divergent* series, a dystopian trilogy that has captured the imaginations of millions of readers around the world. The series, which includes *Divergent*, *Insurgent*, and *Allegiant*, has been adapted into a successful film franchise. Roth has since expanded her writing to include other novels such as *Carve the Mark* and its sequel *The Fates Divide*, exploring new worlds and ideas. Her works often delve into themes of identity, choice, and the complexities of human nature. Roth’s writing continues to inspire readers and is celebrated for its thought-provoking narratives and strong, courageous characters. She currently resides in Chicago, Illinois.
