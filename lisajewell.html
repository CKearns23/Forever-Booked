Lisa Jewell

Lisa Jewell was born in London in 1968. Her first novel, *Ralph's Party*, was the best-selling debut novel of 1999. Since then, she has written over twenty novels, most recently a number of dark psychological thrillers, including *The Girls*, *Then She Was Gone*, *The Family Upstairs*, and *The Night She Disappeared*. Lisa is a New York Times and Sunday Times number one bestselling author who has been published worldwide in over twenty-five languages. Her books are known for their emotional depth, well-drawn characters, and intricate plots. Lisa lives in north London with her husband, two teenage daughters, and the best dog in the world.
