Adam L.G. Nevill

Adam L.G. Nevill is a British author renowned for his atmospheric and terrifying works in the horror genre. His writing blends supernatural elements with psychological suspense, often exploring themes of isolation, folklore, and ancient evil. Nevill’s books, including The Ritual, Last Days, and No One Gets Out Alive, have earned critical acclaim for their chilling narratives and immersive worlds. With a talent for creating oppressive, unsettling atmospheres, Nevill has become a prominent voice in modern horror. His work, often compared to classic horror writers, has garnered a dedicated following and been adapted for film and television.
