Tessa Bailey

Tessa Bailey is a New York Times bestselling author known for her steamy, heartwarming contemporary romance novels. Her works, including *It Happened One Summer*, *Fix Her Up*, and *Hook, Line, and Sinker*, feature sizzling chemistry, quirky characters, and fast-paced storytelling. Tessa’s books often explore themes of love, growth, and second chances, with a heavy dose of humor and emotional depth. With a passion for creating characters readers can’t help but root for, she’s become a go-to author for readers looking for romance with heart and heat.
