Rupi Kaur

Rupi Kaur is a Canadian poet, illustrator, and artist best known for her short and impactful poetry. Her debut collection, *Milk and Honey*, became an international bestseller and has since been translated into over 30 languages. Kaur's works often explore themes of love, trauma, healing, femininity, and self-empowerment, resonating with readers worldwide. Known for her simple yet profound writing style, Kaur's poetry often includes personal illustrations that add depth to her words. Her other bestselling works include *The Sun and Her Flowers*, *Home Body*, *Healing Through Words*, *The Moon and Her Stars*, and *#MeToo*. Rupi Kaur's poetry continues to inspire and touch the hearts of readers around the globe.
