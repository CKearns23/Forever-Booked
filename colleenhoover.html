Colleen Hoover

Colleen Hoover is a New York Times bestselling author known for her emotional and often heart-wrenching novels. She writes contemporary romance and young adult fiction, with popular books like *It Ends with Us*, *Verity*, and *Ugly Love*. Hoover's writing is praised for its raw honesty, complex characters, and ability to capture the depths of love and loss. Her works often tackle difficult themes such as abuse, addiction, and mental health, making her books both relatable and impactful. Hoover’s fanbase continues to grow, and her novels have inspired many adaptations for film and television.
