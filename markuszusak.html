Markus Zusak

Markus Zusak is an Australian author best known for his international bestseller, *The Book Thief*. Published in 2005, *The Book Thief* went on to become one of the most beloved novels of the 21st century, translated into over 40 languages and adapted into a feature film. Zusak's works often explore themes of love, loss, and the power of words. His other notable works include *I Am the Messenger*, *Bridge of Clay*, and *The Underdog*. Known for his unique narrative style and emotionally resonant stories, Zusak is one of the most influential voices in contemporary literature. He currently lives in Sydney, Australia.
