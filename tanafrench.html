Tana French

Tana French is the New York Times bestselling author of *In the Woods*, *The Likeness*, *Faithful Place*, *Broken Harbor*, *The Secret Place*, *The Trespasser*, and *The Witch Elm*. Her books have won prestigious awards, including the Edgar, Anthony, Macavity, and Barry Awards, as well as the Los Angeles Times Award for Best Mystery/Thriller, and the Irish Book Award for Crime Fiction. French’s works are renowned for their intricate plots, compelling characters, and atmospheric settings, often exploring themes of identity, memory, and the dark side of human nature. Her psychological thrillers have captivated readers worldwide. She resides in Dublin, Ireland, with her family.
